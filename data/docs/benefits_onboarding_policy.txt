EMPLOYEE BENEFITS & ONBOARDING POLICY - ACME TECHNOLOGIES PVT LTD
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Document Version: 1.4

1. HEALTH INSURANCE
All full-time employees are covered under a group health insurance plan from day one of employment, covering the employee, spouse, and up to 2 children. The sum insured is INR 5,00,000 per family floater. Employees can opt for enhanced coverage at their own cost through the HR portal during the annual enrollment window.

2. PROBATION PERIOD
New employees undergo a probation period of 3 months from their date of joining. Confirmation of employment is subject to a satisfactory performance review conducted by the reporting manager in the final 2 weeks of probation. Probation may be extended by up to 3 months in exceptional cases.

3. ONBOARDING PROCESS
New hires receive a welcome email with their employee ID, IT credentials, and a checklist 2 business days before their joining date. Day 1 includes a HR orientation session, IT setup, and introduction to the reporting manager and team. A buddy from the same team is assigned to help the new hire navigate their first 30 days.

4. NOTICE PERIOD
The standard notice period is 60 days for employees confirmed after probation, and 15 days during probation. The company may offer buyout of the notice period at its discretion, calculated on the employee's last drawn basic salary.

5. PROVIDENT FUND AND GRATUITY
The company contributes to the Employee Provident Fund (EPF) as per statutory requirements. Gratuity is payable to employees who have completed 5 or more years of continuous service, calculated as per the Payment of Gratuity Act.

6. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Formal performance reviews are conducted twice a year, in April and October. Reviews include a self-assessment, manager assessment, and a calibration discussion. Ratings directly influence annual increments and bonus payouts.

7. REFERRAL BONUS
Employees referring a successful candidate are eligible for a referral bonus of INR 25,000 for standard roles and INR 50,000 for specialized/senior roles, payable after the referred candidate completes 3 months of employment.

8. EXIT PROCESS
Upon resignation, employees must complete a knowledge transfer plan, return all company assets, and complete exit clearance from IT, Finance, and HR. The full and final settlement is processed within 45 days of the last working day.

9. TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT
Employees are entitled to an annual learning budget of INR 20,000 for certifications, courses, or conferences relevant to their role, subject to manager approval.

10. REMOTE WORK EQUIPMENT
Employees in fully remote roles are provided a one-time home office setup allowance of INR 15,000, covering items such as desks, chairs, and monitors, to be claimed within the first 60 days of joining.
