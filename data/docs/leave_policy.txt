LEAVE POLICY - ACME TECHNOLOGIES PVT LTD
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Document Version: 2.3

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
All full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, accrued at a rate of 1.5 days per month. Annual leave must be requested at least 5 working days in advance through the HR portal, except in emergency situations. A maximum of 6 unused annual leave days can be carried forward to the next calendar year. Any leave beyond this carry-forward limit will be forfeited on March 31st.

2. SICK LEAVE
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. For sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days, a medical certificate from a registered practitioner must be submitted within 3 working days of returning to work. Sick leave does not carry forward to the next year and cannot be encashed.

3. MATERNITY LEAVE
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of fully paid maternity leave, in line with statutory requirements. This can be availed starting up to 8 weeks before the expected delivery date. Employees must inform their manager and HR at least 8 weeks before the leave start date to ensure smooth handover planning.

4. PATERNITY LEAVE
Male employees are entitled to 2 weeks of fully paid paternity leave, to be availed within 3 months of the child's birth or adoption.

5. WORK FROM HOME (WFH) POLICY
Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week, subject to manager approval. Requests should be submitted through the HR portal at least 1 day in advance. Roles requiring on-site presence (e.g., facilities, security) are not eligible for WFH.

6. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
Employees are entitled to 5 days of paid bereavement leave in the event of the death of an immediate family member (spouse, parent, child, sibling).

7. UNPAID LEAVE
Employees who have exhausted all paid leave categories may apply for unpaid leave of up to 30 days per year, subject to manager and HR approval. Unpaid leave exceeding 15 consecutive days requires VP-level sign-off.

8. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
The company observes 12 public holidays per year as per the official holiday calendar published in January each year on the HR portal.

9. LEAVE ENCASHMENT
Unused annual leave beyond the carry-forward limit is not encashed. Leave encashment is only permitted at the time of full and final settlement upon resignation or termination, calculated at the employee's last drawn basic salary.

10. APPROVAL HIERARCHY
All leave requests are first routed to the direct reporting manager. If not actioned within 2 working days, the request is auto-escalated to the HR Business Partner for the department.
